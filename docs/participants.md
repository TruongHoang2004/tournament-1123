# 🏸 1123 Badminton Internal Tournament Rules

## 1. Overview

The tournament is organized for members of **1123 Badminton** with a total of **36 players**.

### Player Distribution

- **24 Male Players**, divided into 4 skill levels:
  - Level 1 (Highest)
  - Level 2
  - Level 3
  - Level 4 (Lowest)

- **12 Female Players**, divided into 2 skill levels:
  - Level 1 (Higher)
  - Level 2 (Lower)

---

## 2. Team Composition

Each team will compete across **6 match categories**:

### 🟦 Men's Doubles

1. **Advanced Men's Doubles**
   - Combination: Level 1 + Level 2

2. **Mixed-Level Men's Doubles**
   - Combination: Level 1 + Level 4  
   _or_  
   - Level 2 + Level 3

3. **Intermediate Men's Doubles**
   - Combination: Level 3 + Level 4

---

### 🟪 Mixed Doubles

1. **Advanced Mixed Doubles**
   - Combination: (Level 1 or Level 2 Male) + Level 1 Female

2. **Intermediate Mixed Doubles**
   - Combination: (Level 3 or Level 4 Male) + Level 2 Female

---

### 🟨 Women's Doubles

1. **Women's Doubles**
   - Combination: Female + Female

---

## 3. Participation Rules

- Each player is allowed to participate in **a maximum of 2 match categories**.
- Teams must ensure proper player distribution according to skill levels.
- No player can exceed the match participation limit under any circumstance.

---
