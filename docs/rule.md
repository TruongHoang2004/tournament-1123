# 🏸 1123 Badminton Tournament Rules

## 1. Competition Format

### 1.1. Group Stage

- 6 teams are divided into **2 groups (A & B)**, each group has 3 teams.
- Round-robin format within each group.

### ⚠️ Match Structure (Important)

- Each match category is played as an **independent match timeline**.
- There is **no single team-vs-team match**.
- Each pair competes separately and earns points for their team.

➡️ A team’s total score is the **sum of all match points** earned across all categories.

- In each category, every pair plays **2 matches**.

#### Scoring System

- Win: **2 points**
- Loss: **0 points**

➡️ The **top 2 teams from each group** advance to the Semi-finals.

---

### 1.2. Semi-finals

- Knockout format:
  - Group A Winner vs Group B Runner-up
  - Group B Winner vs Group A Runner-up

### ⚠️ Match Structure

- Matches remain **independent per category**.
- Team advancement is determined by **total accumulated points**, not a single match.

#### Scoring System

- Win: **3 points**
- Loss: **1 point**

---

### 1.3. Final

- The 2 winning teams from the Semi-finals compete to determine the champion.

### ⚠️ Match Structure

- All categories are played independently.
- Final ranking is based on **total accumulated points**.

#### Scoring System

- Win: **4 points**
- Loss: **2 points**

---

## 2. Awards & Ranking System

### 2.1. Category Champions 🥇

- Each match category (e.g. Men's Doubles, Mixed Doubles, etc.) will have its own champion.
- Ranking is determined based on match results within that category.
- The pair/team with the best performance in each category will be awarded.

---

### 2.2. Team Championship 🏆

- Each team accumulates points from all matches played by its members across all categories and rounds.
- Points are calculated based on the scoring system of each round.

➡️ **Total Team Score = Sum of all match points earned by that team**

- The team with the highest total score will be crowned **Overall Champion**.

---

## 3. Schedule

- Official match day: **May 17, 2026 (Sunday)**

---

## 4. Participation Rules

- Each player can participate in a maximum of **2 match categories**.
- Teams must ensure proper lineup according to player levels and category requirements.

---

## 5. Notes

- All matches must follow the official schedule provided by the organizers.
- Players must be present on time; late arrivals may result in forfeiture.
- Any disputes will be resolved by the organizing committee.
- Fair play and sportsmanship are expected at all times.
